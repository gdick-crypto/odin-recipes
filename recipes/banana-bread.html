Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread at its best. Lots of bananas and a hint of almond make a delicious banana bread.

Per Serving: 380 calories; protein 5.8g; carbohydrates 61.2g; fat 13.3g; cholesterol 77mg; sodium 554.4mg.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 5 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 3 drops almond extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a loaf pan with cooking spray.
  2. Whisk flour, salt, and baking soda together in a bowl.
  3. Mix bananas, brown sugar, butter, and almond extract together in a separate bowl. Add flour mixture; mix just until flour is incorporated, about 1 minute. Transfer mixture to the prepared loaf pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Let cool before serving, about 30 minutes.
